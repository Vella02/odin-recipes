Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description

A Peanut Butter and Jelly sandwich is a longtime snack or light lunch for American kids and adults. The ingredients are simple and a PB&J can be made in little time.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Jelly (Strawberry or Grape)
  • Peanut Butter

Steps

  1. lay out two slices of bread
  2. spread peanut butter on one slice
  3. spread jelly on the other slice of bread
  4. Combine the two slices of bread
  5. Enjoy!
